My Name is Patrick Eseme, and I am a Comedian

The thin structured Patrick Eseme was born in 1997 in a small town in Busia,Kenya. He is undeniably one of the fastest rising comedians in kenya after having Established links to many platforms not only in kenya but beyond the frontier.

Patrick Eseme started comedy at a young age of 10 yrs where he used to make hilarious stunts that left everyone's mouth Ajar.He stepped first on the stage in a church podium where he showcased mighty Courage and exposed his funny side. The congregants salute him terming him one of a kind.

He took his comedy to another level when he joined Nation Drama Festivals during inception of Stand up comedy category and won the Funniest Regional Comedian Award In Nakuru.

He is a super Mcee,Actor and a hypnotist who can make you laugh till you forget your troubles.
